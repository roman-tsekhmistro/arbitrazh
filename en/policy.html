Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store and protect your personal information that you provide through our website.

    We collect the following types of information:

    • Personal Information: First name, last name, email, phone number, address.

    The information collected is used for the following purposes:

    • To provide and improve our services.

  • User rights

    You have the right:

    Our website uses cookies to improve your user experience. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or notify you when cookies are sent.

    We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page. We recommend that you periodically review this Policy for up-to-date information.
